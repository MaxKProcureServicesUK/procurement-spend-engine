Year-on-year · 2024–25 full year
What has changed and why
Like-for-like period. December 2024 (£3.34M) excluded. All movements interpreted through the Decision Integrity Framework.
Interpreting the contraction
Spend fell 10.9% and lines fell 15.3% — but average transaction value rose 5.2%. The portfolio is running fewer, higher-value transactions. This is the signature of milestone-based subcontract billing replacing high-frequency material and labour procurement. It is a phase signal, not a volume decline caused by demand reduction.

Minor Bytes · Decision Integrity Framework
Enrichment that increases confidence
Minor Bytes are data characteristics that do not block analysis but would improve precision or depth if addressed. They are not errors. They are opportunities.
Framework position
The Decision Integrity Framework operates on the principle that most insight comes from a relatively small amount of decision-grade data. The analysis above is based on 80% spend coverage, like-for-like period comparison, and GBP base currency. None of the Minor Bytes above prevent the directional conclusions from being drawn. Each would sharpen a specific dimension if addressed — but none is a prerequisite.
